## Brand Read

- Name/positioning: 헬스독 수원점, 안심입양센터, 강아지가정분양.
- Core promise: 건강하게 관리된 아이, 책임감 있는 분양, 평생의 인연.
- Practical hooks: 24시간 상담, 평균 분양가 5~30, 허가번호 공개, 카카오 후기와 블로그 리뷰 수.
- Location proof: 경기 수원시 영통구 대학1로8번길 73 1층.
- Main species/breeds seen: 말티푸, 포메라니안, 푸들, 미니비숑, 닥스훈트, 콩시츄, 꼬숑, 프렌치불독, 고양이.

## Visual Read

- Images are mostly square puppy portraits, warm indoor staging, cream fur rugs, wicker baskets, plants, soft yellow/green props.
- The strongest visual language is cute and reassuring rather than luxury-heavy.
- The current source assets suggest a warm cream/soft green/clean white palette with clear puppy-first cards.

## Content Pattern

- Repeated persuasive phrases:
  - 건강을 최우선으로 생각하는
  - 건강하게 관리된 2개월령 아가들
  - 책임감 있게 분양
  - 아이의 성향과 보호자 생활 환경에 맞게 안내
  - 단순한 분양이 아닌 평생 함께할 가족을 만나는 과정
- Blog post formula:
  - breed keyword title
  - personality and 특징
  - 관리 포인트 or 가격/분양 정보
  - 건강 상태/식사/활동 상태
  - 상담 CTA

## Recommended Site Structure

1. Hero: "건강하게 자란 반려가족을 만나는 공간" plus Kakao CTA, phone CTA, average price, 24h badge.
2. Trust Bar: official license, 24h operation, Kakao reviews 56, blog reviews 228, address.
3. Available Puppies: square image grid with breed, sex/status, personality, health note, 상담하기.
4. Care Promise: health check, environment, temperament matching, after-adoption guidance.
5. Review Proof: Kakao review snippets and blog review cards.
6. Breed Guide: SEO-friendly cards based on blog categories and keywords.
7. Visit/Contact: address, phone, Kakao channel, Instagram, Naver blog, parking/companion access notes.
8. Sticky Mobile CTA: 카톡문의, 전화상담, 길찾기.

## Direction

Do not make it feel like a generic premium dog boutique. Make it feel like a trustworthy local adoption center with cute, warm, real puppy proof. The visual hook should be "real healthy puppies in a gentle care space"; the business hook should be "transparent, 24-hour, licensed, review-backed consultation."
